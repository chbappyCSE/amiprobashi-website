About Us

Ami Probashi is a Government approved Mobile App and Web Portal that assists aspiring Bangladeshi migrants in gaining employment abroad by engaging all the stakeholders. Users can complete mandatory government processes from the comfort of their homes and avoid middlemen, save cost, and gain autonomy within the migration process.

The Ami Probashi Recruitment Management System (A-RMS) makes government processes faster and easier to complete for Recruiting agents and foreign employers via digital completion of attestation, candidate selection, Pre-departure Orientation (PDO) booking, Bureau of Manpower, Employment and Training(BMET) clearance, and digital smart card issuance.

Services for Aspiring Migrants

Employer Portal

Overseas employers can apply for attestation to Bangladesh High Commissions, select and communicate with recruiting agencies, and track their application using their designated Employer portal. They can also view, filter, and select potential candidates.

Government Portal

Government Portal

The Government of Bangladesh can view, monitor, and approve attestation applications, from employers and RAs alike. They can also view and approve migrant applications such as BMET registration, WEWB membership and BMET clearance. Data analytics for training courses, division wise expatriates, and other key analytics are also available via their portal.

Sign in

Recruiting Agency (RA) Portal

A-RMS allows recruiting agencies to post and manage jobs, that directly reach millions of aspiring migrants. RAs can view and filter through prospective employees, chat with applicants and ultimately select their preferred candidates. After they have selected their candidates, they can complete bulk BMET and WEWB registrations, PDO appointment booking, and BMET clearance through the portal. RAs can also apply, track, and obtain attestation, all through the portal.

Embassy Portal

Embassy Portal

Bangladeshi embassies can view attestation applications as well as approve and submit attestation to the ministry through the portal. Embassies can arrange site visits through Ami Probashi to determine the suitability of recruiter. Embassies are able to securely make payments for attestation applications.

Technical Training Center Portal

All the Technical Training Centers (TTC) of Bangladesh have been integrated within the system. TTCs can create batch for certain training courses, enroll students, digitally approve certificates, launch PDO sessions accordingly.

App Features

BMET Registration

Aspiring Migrants can register into the Government mandated BMET databank using the Ami Probashi app.

Priority Vaccination

Priority COVID vaccination through the Surokkha app is available after completing BMET registration.

Job Search

Users can search and apply for authenticated jobs abroad.

Training Courses

Users can search and apply to training courses offered by BMET and BRAC and receive QR-coded digital certificate.

Pre-departure Orientation

Users can enroll for the mandatory PDO training before flying using their BMET number.

WEWB Membership

Users can apply for WEWB membership by themselves through the app.

Help Center

Ami Probashi offers 24/7 dedicated call center and live chat support available.

Approved Offices

Details regarding the nearest Government Offices, Medical Centers, BRAC Migration Center and Bangladesh Embassies abroad.
