About Us

Ami Probashi is a Government approved Mobile App and Web Portal that assists aspiring Bangladeshi migrants in gaining employment abroad by engaging all the stakeholders. Users can complete mandatory government processes from the comfort of their homes and avoid middlemen, save cost, and gain autonomy within the migration process.

The Ami Probashi Recruitment Management System (A-RMS) makes government processes faster and easier to complete for Recruiting agents and foreign employers via digital completion of attestation, candidate selection, Pre-departure Orientation (PDO) booking, Bureau of Manpower, Employment and Training (BMET) clearance, and digital smart card issuance.

Government Portal

The Government of Bangladesh can view, monitor, and approve attestation applications, from employers and recruiting agencies alike. They can also view and approve migrant applications such as BMET registration, WEWB membership and BMET clearance. Data analytics for training courses, division wise expatriates, and other key analytics are also available via their portal.

Recruiting Agency (RA) Portal

A-RMS allows recruiting agencies to post and manage jobs, that directly reach millions of aspiring migrants. Recruiting agencies can view and filter through prospective employees, chat with applicants and ultimately select their preferred candidates. After they have selected their candidates, they can complete bulk BMET and Wage Earners' Welfare Board (WEWB) registrations, PDO appointment booking, and BMET clearance through the portal. Recruiting agencies As can also apply, track, and obtain attestation, all through the portal.

Embassy Portal

Bangladeshi embassies can view attestation applications as well as approve and submit attestation to the ministry through the portal. Embassies can arrange site visits through Ami Probashi to determine the suitability of the recruiter. Embassies are able to securely make payments for attestation applications. (Coming soon)

App Features

BMET Registration

Aspiring Migrants can register into the Government mandated BMET databank using the Ami Probashi app.

Priority Vaccination

Priority COVID vaccination through the Surokkha app is available after completing BMET registration.

Job Search

Users can search and apply for authenticated jobs abroad.

Training Courses

Users can search and apply to training courses offered by BMET and BRAC and receive QR-coded digital certificate.

Pre-departure Orientation

Users can enroll for the mandatory PDO training before flying using their BMET number. (Coming soon)

WEWB Membership

Users can apply for WEWB membership by themselves through the app. (Coming soon)

Help Center

Ami Probashi offers 24/7 dedicated call center and live chat support.

Approved Offices

Details regarding the nearest Government Offices, Medical Centers, BRAC service points and Bangladesh Embassies abroad.

Value-Added Services

Checklist, Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ), Country specific guidelines etc.
